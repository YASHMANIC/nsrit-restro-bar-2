El Dorado Rum

The rums by El Dorado are actually remarkable blends of selected vintage rums. The aged rums are created on the banks of the Demerara River in Guyana, and have been awarded by numerous titles, all of which indicate one thing, El Dorado is one of the best rum brands in India, along with the globe. Aged in former Bourbon casks, El Dorado rums are uniquely complex and distinctly flavourful.

Mount Gay Rum

Made from molasses and water filtered by natural coral, Mount Gay rums are recognised by every rum enthusiast across the world. The most famous variant arguably is Mount Gay Barbados Eclipse, known for its fruity and well-rounded palate, boasting of enticing notes of banana, vanilla and toasty oak. A sip of this excellent rum carries subtle hints of spices, that make for a mouthful of delicate and delightful dark spirit.

Malibu Rum

One of the best-selling rum brands in India and the world at large, Malibu's Coconut flavoured liqueur is absolutely mouthwatering. It's like a vacation in a bottle, one that's full of tropical fresh coconut flavours with notes of vanilla custard. More than anything, the stuff goes down remarkably easily, considering its sweet fruity taste and charming coconut aroma. One of the best rum brands we've ever tried, Malibu rum can be downed just by itself, or by throwing in some pineapple juice or flavour liquor.

Appleton Estate Rum

The Signature Blend by Appleton Estate is touted to be amongst the best rums in India, and one sip of it's fruity, tasty flavours later, it becomes starkly clear why. Imagine some alcoholic salted caramel, and you've got a pretty good idea of how Appleton Estate's Signature Blend rum tastes. It's a pot-distilled Jamaican rum, with a super smooth and bold profile of flavours, and the palate has lovely notes of oak, nutmeg and exotic spices.

Bacardi Rum

Bacardi is indisputably one of the best rum brands in India, one that offers an assortment of exciting flavours to its eager customers. In addition to the terrific fruity flavours such as Apple, Strawberry, Limon, Grapefruit, Orange and Raspberry, Bacardi also has a white, black and gold rum up for grabs. Their unique blends come with remarkable taste and fragrant aromas, and while the gold variant is certainly one of our favourites, we'd suggest you try all before settling on yours

Captain Morgan Rum

Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum is a true classic in the category of the best rum brands in India. One of the most top rated rums for mixing in splendid cocktails, this one boasts of rich flavours of vanilla, brown sugar and aromatic spices, topped off with a hint of oak and a beautifully balanced, smooth finish. It's an affordable rum brand, that also tastes great, and blends wonderfully well into fancy drinks.

Old Monk Rum

Now, this iconic rum brand doesn't exactly need an introduction, does it? Extremely budget-friendly yet effective, Old Monk has been the most adored dark rum in India since 1954. Known for its soft taste palate, comprising vanilla, cola, spice, roasted coffee beans, and caramel flavours, Old Monk is cheap, accessible, and absolutely yummy by itself, with some water, or a soft drink mixer, all in equal measure.

McDowell's NO1 Rum

Not just one of the top selling rum brands in India, but McDowell's Celebration rum has been amongst the best selling rum brands in the world for a while now. It has a distinct taste of sharp vanilla and caramelised sugar, with subtle flavours of tea leaves, citrus zest and dry tobacco rounding off the palate. Contessa Rum

This one offers a decently smooth and bold taste, which at this price makes this rum a total steal. The flavours are warm and mellow, and Contessa rum boasts of a pronounced woody taste. This cheap rum brand in India, has won several awards for its quality, including a Bronze at the International Wine & Spirit Competition 2007 held in the UK.

Havana Club Rum

The most popular Havana Club rum is the Anejo 3-year-old, that's aged for three years in bourbon casks, and comes with a clean round palate comprising fresh notes of citrus, oak, vanilla, some spice and lemon. One of the best budget-friendly rum brands in India, this one doesn't compromise on the taste or quality one bit. It's one of the most distinguished light Cuban rums, perfect for pouring into any rum cocktail, throwing in with some mixer, or just a few blocks of ice, all in equal measure.
